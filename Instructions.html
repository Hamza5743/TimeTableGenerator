Time Table Generator For FAST - NUCES

Use it to generate clash less timetables according to the times you want a break and the courses+sections you want to choose It will give you all the possible time tables according to your schedule. Click on the link to give it a go.

Or visit the Repo.

Features

  • Clashless Time Table.
  • Interactive design for adding breaks.
  • Shows Multiple possibilities.
  • Ability to select multiple sections for each course.

How To Use

  1. Choose all the courses that you want to study.

  1. Check your desired sections from the table.

  1. Check your desired timeslots from the bottom table.

  1. Press the GENERATE button.

  2. If there are no clashes the site will generate schedule(s) under the generate button. scroll down to see the tables.
